We Are Panveyo

We help businesses thrive with cutting-edge tools built to simplify operations, improve revenue, and deliver the best customer experience — especially for restaurants and retailers.

Our Journey

From an ambitious startup to a full-suite business platform powering growth across India.

When Panveyo first launched, our goal was simple: support businesses with tools they actually need. We soon realized that most local businesses lacked tech to handle their daily operations — no reliable billing, poor stock tracking, and manual work everywhere.

So we built a modular platform that fits businesses of all sizes — whether it’s a single café or a multi-chain brand. Over time, we’ve grown into a robust ecosystem trusted by thousands of businesses across the country.
